# DATA RETENTION, DELETION AND ARCHIVAL POLICY

**Effective Date:** 11/06/2026

This Data Retention, Deletion and Archival Policy ("Policy") describes how F2 Fintech Private Limited, operating the FinHeal platform ("FinHeal", "Company", "we", "our", or "us"), collects, stores, retains, archives, deletes, and manages personal information and credit-related information of users.

This Policy should be read together with the Privacy Policy, Terms of Use, and Credit Information Consent & Authorization.

### PURPOSE OF THIS POLICY

The purpose of this Policy is to:

- Establish data retention standards.
- Define data deletion procedures.
- Protect user privacy.
- Comply with applicable laws and regulations.
- Meet obligations imposed by Credit Information Companies (CICs), lenders, service providers, and regulatory authorities.
- Reduce risks associated with excessive data storage.

### SCOPE

This Policy applies to:

- Website users
- Mobile application users
- Prospective customers
- Existing customers
- Credit score users
- Credit report users
- Loan marketplace users
- Customer support interactions

It applies to all information collected by FinHeal through any channel.

### DATA RETENTION PRINCIPLES

FinHeal follows the following retention principles:

a. Data shall be retained only for legitimate business purposes.
b. Data shall not be retained longer than necessary.
c. Retention periods shall comply with legal and regulatory requirements.
d. Data shall be securely deleted after expiration of retention periods.
e. Retention periods may be extended where required by law, court orders, regulatory investigations, fraud prevention requirements, or dispute resolution obligations.

### CREDIT REPORT RETENTION

Credit Reports and Credit Scores obtained from Credit Information Companies may be retained for up to forty-five (45) days from the date of retrieval.

After expiration of the applicable retention period:

- Credit Reports shall be deleted,
- anonymized,
- archived in compliance with applicable regulations,
- or otherwise disposed of in accordance with bureau agreements and applicable law.

Where a shorter retention period is required by a bureau agreement, regulatory requirement, or law, the shorter period shall prevail.

### USER ACCOUNT INFORMATION

User account information may be retained while the account remains active.

Upon account closure:

- Active access may be disabled immediately.
- Certain information may continue to be retained to comply with legal, regulatory, audit, fraud prevention, or dispute resolution requirements.

### CONSENT RECORDS

Consent records may be retained for audit and compliance purposes.

Consent records may include:

- Consent text version
- Date and time of consent
- IP address
- Device details
- Authentication logs
- User identifier
- Consent withdrawal records

Such records may be retained for periods required under applicable laws and regulatory requirements.

### LOAN MARKETPLACE DATA

Loan application and eligibility-related information may be retained for:

- Customer servicing
- Fraud prevention
- Regulatory compliance
- Audit purposes
- Partner lender obligations

Retention periods may vary depending upon applicable legal requirements and lender agreements.

### COMMUNICATION RECORDS

Customer communications may be retained for:

- Customer support
- Complaint resolution
- Quality assurance
- Regulatory compliance
- Fraud investigations

Communication records may include:

- Emails
- Calls
- Chat transcripts
- WhatsApp interactions
- Support tickets

### MARKETING DATA

Marketing preferences and communication records may be retained until:

- User withdrawal of consent,
- account deletion,
- or expiration of the applicable business purpose.

Users may opt out of promotional communications at any time.

### LEGAL AND REGULATORY RETENTION

Certain information may be retained beyond standard retention periods where necessary to:

- Comply with laws.
- Respond to legal notices.
- Defend legal claims.
- Meet regulatory obligations.
- Prevent fraud.
- Support investigations.
- Enforce contractual rights.

### USER REQUESTS FOR DELETION

Users may request deletion of personal information by contacting:

**wecare@f2fintech.com**

FinHeal may verify the identity of the requesting individual before processing deletion requests.

Deletion requests may be denied where retention is legally required.

### ACCOUNT DELETION

Users may request closure of their FinHeal account.

Following successful verification:

- User access may be terminated.
- Personal information may be deleted or anonymized.
- Data required by law may continue to be retained.

Account deletion does not automatically remove records that FinHeal is legally obligated to retain.

### DATA DELETION METHODS

Depending on data type and storage location, FinHeal may:

- Permanently delete data,
- securely erase data,
- anonymize data,
- archive data,
- overwrite data,
- destroy physical records.

Deletion methods shall be selected based on risk, sensitivity, and regulatory requirements.

### DATA ARCHIVAL

Where permitted by law, certain information may be archived for:

- Audit purposes
- Compliance requirements
- Historical records
- Fraud investigations
- Legal defense

Archived data shall be subject to restricted access controls.

### SECURITY OF RETAINED DATA

FinHeal shall implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect retained information against:

- Unauthorized access
- Accidental loss
- Unauthorized disclosure
- Alteration
- Misuse
- Destruction

Access shall be limited to authorized personnel with a legitimate business need.

### CROSS-BORDER DATA TRANSFERS

FinHeal primarily operates within India.

If data is transferred outside India through service providers, cloud providers, or technology partners, appropriate contractual, legal, and security safeguards shall be implemented as required under applicable law.
